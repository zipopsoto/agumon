How to Apply Promo Codes

  1. Select your hotel and click 'Book now' or 'Add to cart'.
  2. After adding to cart, a confirmation will appear at the top right.
  3. Click 'Book now' again to move to the payment screen.
  4. On the right side of the payment screen, find the 'Promotions' section.
    Choose from the dropdown or enter the code manually in the 'Enter a promo code' field.
    â€» Be sure to use the promo input field on the payment page, not on the public promo listing page.

  6. Some codes apply automatically during checkout.
  7. Promo codes may not work on special deals or restricted properties.
  8. Always check the expiration date and usage terms for each code.
